Main Characters (Act I - His Machiavellian Grace)
Monika Iris

Species:
Demon

About:
Birthed from the Ink Machine and dimension hopper. One of the few survivors of The Great Indoctrination. No known family. Was raised within the ministry of Ghost as a Sister of Sin. Abandoned this life when she met her now-living companion: Bowie. Has been living in the Kaisarion (a bunker) for a couple of years now, scraping to survive. Descendant of the demons who once walked Satania by design.

Appearence (Extract):
“... she put forward her half naked arms, being only hugged by a short-sleeved jacket and arm warmers with inverted crosses on them. Her purple-blue color-coded outfit catching the eye of anyone she came across, her bewitching, heterochromia gaze would leave most mute, one enchanting brown eye and one horridly pretty, white eye.”

Recorded Abilities:
Naturally, she can take flight with her wings. Astonishing withstand of interdimensional travel, but not consciously or on purpose. When the specimen loses temper they seem to transform. Consequences include: Off the charts levels of strength, deformed facial features, black stream of tears running down their face and the ability to summon a magenta fog around them. Self referred to as “Fog Weaver Monika”.

Weapon/s:
The Square Hammer.
Bowie

Species:
Angel

About:
Lone angel who lost her family to the Great Indoctrination. Has great trouble adjusting to the Neon Genesis. Grief-stricken, wounded and bitter, has been living in the Kaisarion (a bunker) for a couple of years now, scraping to survive. She hides her true self from her living companion (she pretends to be human). Has turned to the dark lord for survival but deep down doesn’t condone what she’s doing.

Appearence (Extract):
“A young yet wounded, bitter woman, the nihilistic look on her face said more than her words ever could. Even to an outsider she seemed like a pensative, sensitive person, looking down upon anyone she came across. She styled a bright pastel pink jacket, sporting a slightly darker undershirt. Complemented by her blue, baggy jeans and dark, magenta boots. All culminating in a pair of golden skeleton-like gloves and skeleton-like face paint she chose to put on, being juxtaposed by other decorative face paint in the form of a red lightning strike, all contrasting the inverted cross silver necklace she showcased.”

Recorded Abilities:
Naturally, she can take flight with her wings. Somewhat stable withstand of interdimensional travel but is not able to travel on her own.

Weapon/s:
A shotgun and a Bible.
Aether Marchionne Luminous

Species:
Angel

About:
Eldest son of the most powerful family in Heaven.
Short tempered, a bit paranoid and tends to avoid opening up due to how he was raised. Banished from the Council of Angels and disowned by his father. Has taken the mission to bring back Dei into his own hands. Was poisoned with Damballa’s Mercy and came back as his first stage of ascendance.

Appearence (Extract):
(Post Ascension)
“His frame was wrapped in a white cropped temple garment, rimmed in a delicate wave pattern. A golden sun-crucifix pin clung to his bib, catching the light of Heaven itself. His hands flexed in new gloves, dark red and cropped short at the palms, leaving his lower skin exposed. The undershirt beneath was the same shade—torn open to let forth a new burden: an additional set of wings. Then another. Until he bore five pairs in total—or four and a half would be more accurate, each fanning outward like the rays of a violent sunrise.
His jeans, baggy and gray, were marked with divine insignia: a bold yellow upright cross on the right, a white rose blooming on the left. White-and-gold boots grounded him, gleaming with each step.”

Recorded Abilities:
Can take flight with his wings. Trained swordsman. Reviving himself after death. Unable to die of old age. Animals really like him.

Weapon/s:
The council's axe.

Mikael Avrgrund

Species:
Human turned android

About:
Once a pope for the people of Dei. Now an alchemist working on the side of voodoo Gods against the angel race. Has lost all and any emotion he may have once had. Sickly, purple skin tone due to the fact he was resuscitated after his body was deprived of its blood. Cousin of Juanito and Yose Diaz.

Appearence (Extract):
“His face was sealed behind a welding helmet, visor glinting faintly, but the skin of his neck and hand betrayed a strange purple tone. He wore battered blue armor stamped with the outline of a red shield—absurdly, the crest emblazoned with a papaya. His frame was incomplete: the right arm severed long ago, its remnants still visible. In his remaining hand he clutched a road stop sign as if it were a king’s scepter. And where his legs should have been, there was only fire and steel—he hovered, propelled by the screaming thrust of a rocket embedded into his pelvis, smoke curling in his wake.”

Recorded Abilities:
Hovering by propelling himself. Infiltrate security systems.

Weapon/s:
A stop sign. Damballa’s Mercy.

Yose Diaz

Species:
Thingamajig

About:
An outcast of his own kind hellbent on revenge against all. His plan is an ultimatum to destroy everything and anything. Betraying anyone who helps him to get what he wants.
Cartoonishly evil and very narcissistic.

Appearence (Extract):
"... limbless being whose extremities—hands, feet, head—floated in orbit around his torso, held aloft by an unseen force. Watching him move was like watching a puppet without strings, each part drifting and snapping into place with eerie precision. [...] His skin glowed a sickly, neon yellow, his face dominated by huge pitch-black eyes and heavy brows that floated almost a fraction off his spherical head, exaggerating every flicker of emotion. Unlike his brethren, he had no nose at all—just smooth skin between those void-like eyes.
He dressed in the garish style of a street-king: reddish-gray cap and gloves, a green shirt with jagged white stripes, beige pants cinched with a “V”-shaped buckle, and massive brown boots that seemed to drag his floating form toward earth.
He looked ridiculous and terrifying all at once."

Recorded Abilities:
